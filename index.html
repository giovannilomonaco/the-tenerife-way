Who We Are

The island beyond the tourist trail

The Tenerife Way is a private experience company rooted in a deep love for this island. We curate intimate encounters with Tenerife's nature, cuisine, culture, and hidden corners — experiences you simply cannot find in a travel catalogue.


Every journey is designed around you: your pace, your interests, your sense of wonder. Our local guides bring a lifetime of knowledge, ensuring each experience feels genuine, personal, and unforgettable.

100% Private & Bespoke

What We Offer

Four ways to fall in love
with the island

From volcanic summits to ancient forests, from sun-drenched vineyards to living culture — every corner of Tenerife holds a story worth experiencing in private.

Nature & Volcano

Private sunrise hikes on Mount Teide, guided walks through the Anaga rainforest, and stargazing sessions under one of the world's clearest skies.

Food & Wine

Exclusive dinners in volcanic vineyards, private tastings of Canarian wines and artisan cheeses, and hands-on cooking sessions with local chefs.

Guanche Culture

Private visits to archaeological sites, storytelling sessions with cultural experts, and encounters with craftspeople keeping ancient traditions alive.

Exclusive Stays

Carefully selected rural houses, boutique fincas, and cliff-side retreats — handpicked for their authenticity, privacy, and connection to the landscape.
